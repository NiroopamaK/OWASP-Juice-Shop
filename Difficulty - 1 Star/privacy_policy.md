## OWASP Juice Shop – Privacy Policy Challenge

In the Privacy Policy challenge of OWASP Juice Shop, the objective was simply to locate and access the application’s privacy policy.
The steps to solve this challenge were straightforward. The application was navigated to find sections where legal or informational content is typically located.
By opening the Privacy Policy, which is presented as a Markdown file (`legal.md`), its contents were accessed and the challenge was completed.

## What This Teaches About Security

- **Information accessibility**  
  Important legal documents like privacy policies should be easily accessible to users.

- **No exploitation required**  
  Not all challenges involve vulnerabilities—some focus on awareness and proper navigation.

- **Understanding application structure**  
  Knowing where to look (e.g., About or legal sections) is key when exploring web applications.
